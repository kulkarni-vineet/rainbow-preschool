🌈 आमच्याबद्दल · About Us

Where Every Child's Light Shines Bright ✨

Rainbow Pre-School opened on 14th April 2025 — founded with one heartfelt belief: that learning should be joyful. We are a play-based, activity-driven preschool in Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar built around how children naturally learn best — through play, exploration and communication.

🌈 In Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar, alongside our official name Rainbow Pre-School, we are also lovingly known as Rainbow Kidzone — visit us at rainbowkidzone.com

🕉️ Unique! We teach Sanskrit language to ALL students — from Playgroup to Senior KG. Songs, shlokas and interactive activities make it fun and memorable!

Our 4 fully air-conditioned classrooms and dedicated EV Hall for events and activities make Rainbow Pre-School a truly modern, comfortable and inspiring learning environment.

🎉 शनिवार = मजेचा दिवस! Every Saturday is FunDay — special games, activities and surprises for all students!
